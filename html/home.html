DoughYou Mind Sharing?!

DoughYou is an initiative to reduce waste of food. According to USDA's Economic Research Service, of 31% food loss at the retail and consumer levels, corresponded to approximately 133 billion pounds and $161 billion worth of food in 2010. Consumers contribute to food loss when they buy or cook more than they need and choose to throw out the extras. Wasted food eventually ends up in landfills and breaks down to greenhouse gases.Therefore, we have developed a website that will encourage students by rewarding each time they're able to share their non-perishable food with their peers.

Small habits build up to big changes, instead of implementing the next "big" thing -- we need to take a reflection upon ourselves and be the CHANGE we want to see in this world.
